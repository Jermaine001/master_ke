About us

We are the SCITT class of 2025

As the SCITT class of 2025, we are a community of aspiring church technology professionals committed to integrating faith with innovation. Our mission is to harness the power of technology to enhance worship experiences, foster community engagement, and advance the Kingdom of God. Through hands-on training and a solid theological foundation, we aim to equip ourselves with the skills and knowledge necessary to serve effectively in the evolving landscape of church technology.

SCITT Class of 2025 Gallery

Welcome to the SCITT Class of 2025 Gallery, where we celebrate the dedication, growth, and accomplishments of our teacher trainees. This collection highlights key moments from our journey, including classroom experiences, community engagements, and professional development activities.

Our SCITT program offers a hands-on approach to teacher training, immersing trainees in real classroom settings from day one. This practical experience is complemented by interactive lectures and workshops, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience.
